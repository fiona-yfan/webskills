2. transition : transition-property transition-duration transition-timing-function transtion-delay

  • transition-property:none|all|property 定义转换动画的CSS属性名称
  • transition-duration : 过度效果持续时间
  • transition-timing-function : linear|ease|ease-in|ease-out|ease-in-out|cubic-bezier(n,n,n,n)
    • linear匀速
    ease默认，慢速开始-快速-慢速结束
    ease-in慢速开始
    ease-out慢速结束
    ease-in-out慢速开始和结束
    cubic-bezier在cubic-bezier函数中定义一个0-1的值
  • transition-delay : 延迟时间

3. animations 动画 animation-name: running-girl; animation-duration: 500ms; animation-iteration-count: infinite; animation-timing-function: step-end;

animation vs transition: transition 只定义的开始和结束的样式。animations可以定义了每一贞的样式
animation-name: 规定 @keyframes 动画名称
animation-duration: 规定动画完成一个周期花费的的秒或者毫秒，default 0
animation-timing-function: 规定动画的速度曲线，default ease
animation-delay: 规定动画合适开始，default 0
animation-interation-count: 规定动画播放次数，default 1
animation-direction: 规定动画在下一周期逆向播放，默认normal
animation-play-state: 默认running，规定动画是否正在运行或暂停，paused|running
